About Me

It all started with a trip to SXSW Austin. I was lucky to have the chance to shoot for shows of Hinds, DYGL, etc. I realized how much I enjoyed this so I decided to further pursue it when I went back to New York City, where I base. I've covered Miyavi and other brooklyn indie musicians and the list is still growing.
My music journey started at the age of 6 when I first started to learn the piano. It's been an inseparable part of my life ever since. I primarily focus on classical repertoire but also listen to a lot of garage rock or any kind of rock. A hardcore punker at heart.
